EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Opportunity

TK Steels operates a high-capacity, ISO-certified alloy and carbon steel manufacturing facility in Ludhiana with strong credentials and a solid reputation in the automotive, forging, and fastener industries.

However, the company’s current digital lead capture infrastructure is significantly behind modern B2B buyer expectations. High-intent buyers who visit the website with specific grade requirements, quantity needs, or technical questions currently have no intelligent way to engage — resulting in lost opportunities and slow follow-up.

This prototype demonstrates a practical, high-impact solution: an AI-powered Sales Assistant that can answer technical questions about steel grades in real time and guide buyers through a structured RFQ process, dramatically improving lead capture and qualification.

CURRENT STATE ANALYSIS

Website & Engagement Gaps

Key limitations identified in the May 2026 audit that are costing TK Steels potential business.

No Intelligent Engagement Layer
Zero chatbot or automated response capability. Every inquiry requires manual follow-up, often the next business day.
Basic, Unstructured Enquiry Form
Current form only collects name, email, phone and free-text. No product/grade selector, no quantity, no application, no file upload.
Passive WhatsApp Experience
A floating WhatsApp button exists but has no context, no pre-filled requirements, and no connection to the buyer’s journey on the site.
Poor Technical Buyer Journey
Buyers looking for specific grades (EN24, EN353, SAE 4140, etc.), chemical composition, or test certificates receive no guided support.
Business Impact: High-intent buyers leave without speaking to anyone. Sales team receives unqualified, unstructured inquiries. Response times are slow. Significant revenue is being left on the table.
THE SOLUTION

Proposed AI Sales Assistant

This working prototype demonstrates exactly how an AI assistant can close the gaps identified above.

Grade Intelligence
  • • Answers questions about EN series, SAE grades & equivalents
  • • Provides properties, applications, and comparisons
  • • Available 24×7 for engineers and procurement teams
Structured RFQ Capture
  • • Guided conversational flow (product → grade → size → qty → application)
  • • Collects timeline, special requirements & contact details
  • • Generates clean, professional RFQ summaries with reference numbers
Instant Qualification + Handoff
  • • Pre-qualifies leads before they reach sales
  • • One-click WhatsApp handoff with full context
  • • Ready for CRM integration and auto-routing
